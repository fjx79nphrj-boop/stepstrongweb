The problem

Every tool for stepparents tracks the wrong thing.

Every app for blended families is a co-parenting logistics tool — custody calendars, scheduling with the other parent, expense splitting. They exist to manage the business of shared parenting.

None of them address what actually makes stepparenting hard: the relationship itself. The sustained, invisible effort. The consistent care that gets no feedback. The slow erosion that sets in when you can't tell whether anything you do is landing.

StepStrong is the first tool built for that experience.

What the research says

The timeline is longer than anyone told you.

Full integration takes 7–12 years.

Braithwaite et al.'s longitudinal research found that stepfamily integration — not warmth, not closeness, just functional integration — typically takes 7–12 years. Most stepparents declare failure at year two.

Neutral acceptance is a milestone, not a failure.

Researchers identify "neutral acceptance" as a distinct, positive phase in stepfamily development. It is not the goal falling short — it is a stage on the path. Most stepparents don't know it has a name.

Negativity bias distorts your read of the relationship.

The brain weights negative interactions 3–5× more heavily than positive ones. One rejection dominates a week of small positives. Logging corrects for this — it shows you the actual ratio.

The quiet acts are what they remember.

Adult stepchildren consistently describe not grand gestures but specific, quiet, repeated acts as what shaped their view of their stepparent. The things that felt invisible to you were often what registered most.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What data the app stores

StepStrong stores all your data locally on your device using IndexedDB. This includes interaction logs, profile settings, progress snapshots, and any photos you attach to entries. None of this data is transmitted to any server.

Device backups

StepStrong does not sync your data to any cloud service. Your data is not actively sent anywhere by the app. However, if you use your device's built-in backup feature (such as iCloud Backup on iOS or Google account backup on Android), your operating system may include app data in that backup as part of its normal backup behaviour. This is outside StepStrong's control and applies to most apps on your device.

What data leaves your device

The only data that leaves your device is related to in-app subscription purchases. When you subscribe to StepStrong Premium, your subscription status and an anonymous device identifier are sent to RevenueCat, our subscription management provider. This is used solely to verify and manage your subscription. RevenueCat does not receive any of your journal entries, profile information, or usage data.

You can read RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

No tracking

StepStrong does not track you across other apps or websites. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no third-party data collection beyond the subscription management described above.

No account required

There is no account, no login, and no registration. Your data is not associated with your identity in any way.

Photos

Photos you attach to entries are resized and stored locally on your device as part of your entry data. They are never uploaded anywhere.

Your control

You can export all your data as a JSON file at any time from the Settings screen. You can permanently delete all data from Settings, or by deleting the app.

Children

StepStrong is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top of this page.
