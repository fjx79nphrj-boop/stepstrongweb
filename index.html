An honest app for a hard journey

The path to a functional relationship with a resistant stepchild typically takes 7 to 12 years. No app will change that timeline.

What StepStrong does is make the invisible visible — so you don't abandon what's working, and you don't burn out from effort that feels unseen.

Your data stays on your device
