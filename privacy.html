Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What data the app stores

StepStrong stores all your data locally on your device using IndexedDB. This includes interaction logs, profile settings, progress snapshots, and any photos you attach to entries. None of this data is transmitted to any server.

Device backups

StepStrong does not sync your data to any cloud service. Your data is not actively sent anywhere by the app.

However, if you use your device's built-in backup feature (such as iCloud Backup on iOS or Google account backup on Android), your operating system may include app data in that backup as part of its normal backup behaviour. This is outside StepStrong's control and applies to most apps on your device.

What data leaves your device

The only data that leaves your device is related to in-app subscription purchases. When you subscribe to StepStrong Premium, purchase information and an anonymous device identifier are sent to RevenueCat, our subscription management provider. This is used solely to verify and manage your subscription. RevenueCat does not receive any of your journal entries, profile information, or usage data.

You can read RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

No tracking

StepStrong does not track you across other apps or websites. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no third-party data collection of any kind beyond the subscription management described above.

No account required

There is no account, no login, and no registration. Your data is not associated with your identity in any way.

Photos

Photos you attach to entries are resized and stored locally on your device as part of your entry data. They are never uploaded anywhere.

Your control

You can export all your data as a JSON file at any time from the Settings screen. You can permanently delete all data from Settings, or by deleting the app.

Children

StepStrong is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top of this page.
